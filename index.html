About Us

Last Resort Fabrications specializes in custom sound systems, all forms of electrical work, and vehicle upgrades. We provide precision-crafted solutions for a variety of needs. From custom components to unique upgrades, our skilled team is dedicated to delivering excellence in every project.

Our Services

Custom Sound Systems

We offer a wide range of sound systems to meet your needs, including speakers, tweeters, subwoofers, and amplifiers. We partner with various brands to ensure quality and stay within your price range.

Electrical and Wiring

We sell and install car starters, GPS trackers, gauges, and more. We also handle repairs and upgrades for any electrical issues you may have.

Vehicle Fabrication

Our team is equipped to fabricate any custom parts or enclosures you need. We ensure durability and performance while creating visually impressive designs.
